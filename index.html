1. Introduction

A business hackathon is an intensive event where participants collaborate to develop innovative solutions to business challenges. Unlike coding-focused hackathons, business hackathons emphasize strategic thinking, creative problem-solving, and business model development. This guide provides a step-by-step framework for organizing both intra-university (within a single university) and inter-university (among multiple universities) business hackathons successfully.
